# Source: databases, speedruns, and other versions

Reviewed 2026-07-06. Grab-bag of secondary references.

- [speedrun.com leaderboard](https://www.speedrun.com/buzz_junior_jungle_party) — Singleplayer/Multiplayer categories, PS2/PSP platforms, per-language variables (14 languages; US "American" vs "British" audio) — useful evidence of regional audio differences.
- [PCSX2 wiki](https://wiki.pcsx2.net/Buzz!_Junior:_Jungle_Party) — regional release/serial data.
- [Metacritic](https://www.metacritic.com/game/buzz-junior-jungle-party/) — aggregate 70.
- [Push Square PS3 page](https://www.pushsquare.com/games/ps3/buzz_junior_jungle_party) — PSN release data (5-game version).
- PSP version playthroughs: [video 1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PtHgsZw7CLU), [video 2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZRSzaZLsGK0) — the second lists the PSP's 15 games in its description (includes five of our [starter six](../recreation-notes.md): Bubble Bath, Totem Poles, Jungle Hurdles, Monkey Bomb, Whack A Squirrel).
- PS3 (2008) longplay: [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ixpb1R5MUyc) — usable to identify which 5 games the PSN version kept (open item).
- Speedrun example: [Any% 22:13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M3RRRLHjkjY).
- Dead official sites (check via Wayback Machine): `buzzjunior.com/en_GB/jungleparty/` and `us.playstation.com/buzzjuniorjungleparty/`.
