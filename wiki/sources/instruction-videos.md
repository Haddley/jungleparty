# Source: in-game instruction videos compilation (YouTube)

Reviewed 2026-07-06. ["All Minigames Instructions Videos"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HiTEaKElVco) — the narrated per-mini-game instruction movies from the game disc, compiled by a fan (uploader notes beta variants exist for Odd One Out and Door Running). Video © Sony; described in words only.

These are the movies each mini-game plays before it starts (skippable with the Buzz! button per the [manual](us-instruction-manual.md)). They are the authoritative statement of each game's rules as told to players, and the model for our recreation's **instruction cards**: one short narrated sentence of objective + one of controls.
