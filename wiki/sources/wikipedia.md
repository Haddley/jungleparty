## Key facts

- First game of the **Buzz! Junior** spin-off series (no quiz questions, pure mini-games). Developer Magenta Software; publisher SCEE/SCEA. See [game-overview](../game-overview.md).
- Releases: PS2 EU 20 Oct 2006; PS2 NA 30 Oct 2007; PS3 PSN EU 28 Aug 2008 / NA 12 Mar 2009; PSP ("Jungle Party") EU/AU 5 Nov 2010.
- Content by version: EU PS2 = 40 multiplayer + 10 single-player variants (2 of them SP-exclusive); US PS2 = 25 games; PS3 PSN = 5 games (720p, DualShock-playable); PSP = 15 games.
- Won the **BAFTA British Academy Children's Award 2007** (video game). Metacritic 70.
- Four monkeys named and coloured **Blue, Orange, Green, Yellow**, matching the buzzer's colour buttons — see [monkeys](../monkeys.md).
- Bronze/Silver/Gold costume outfits unlock via high scores.
- Buzz buzzer hardware details from the [series article](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buzz!): four wired handsets merging into one USB plug; later wireless sets use a USB dongle. See [buzz-controller](../buzz-controller.md).
