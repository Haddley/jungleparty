# Source: contemporary and retrospective reviews

Reviewed 2026-07-06. Seven review/retrospective articles; each contributed rule fragments and presentation detail now folded into the concept pages. All text remains at the linked sites; facts paraphrased.

- **[IGN (Nov 2007)](https://www.ign.com/articles/2007/11/06/buzz-junior-jungle-party-review)** — 7/10. Notes the risk/reward design thread across games (Lion Taming, Freefall), praises the "adorably animated" monkeys whose animation states blend seamlessly (a clapping monkey transitions into the same idle as a freshly-zapped one), describes the soundtrack as driving jungle rhythms with heavy chanting. US release needs buzzers from a Mega Quiz bundle. → [monkeys](../monkeys.md), [presentation](../presentation.md).
- **[Wilcox Arcade (2020)](https://www.wilcoxarcade.com/single-post/2020/06/01/buzz-junior-jungle-party-playstation-2-review)** — most detailed retrospective. US session lengths 10/15/20/25 games; controller two-thumb technique (left thumb blue+orange, right thumb green+yellow — confirming the button column order); rules for Card Counting, Rocket Riders, Totem Poles, Splat. → [game-structure](../game-structure.md), [buzz-controller](../buzz-controller.md), [totem-poles](../totem-poles.md).
- **[NoobFeed](https://www.noobfeed.com/reviews/buzz-junior-jungle-party)** — EU version: 40 games; reports session lengths 5/10/20/40 (conflicts with Wilcox's US counts — open item); Crazy Croc and Campfire Chaos descriptions.
- **[Kikizo (2006)](https://games.kikizo.com/reviews/ps2/buzzjunior_jungleparty.asp)** — launch review: Hippo Splash spot-the-difference detail, Lethal Lily Pads coconut knock-offs, costume unlocks via single player.
- **[Sydney Morning Herald (2006)](https://www.smh.com.au/technology/buzz-junior-jungle-party-20061031-gdopsu.html)** — buzzer hardware description ("a red Temptation-style buzzer on top"), Bubble Bath humour, AU pricing with/without buzzers.
- **[Common Sense Media](https://www.commonsensemedia.org/game-reviews/buzz-junior-jungle-party)** — E10+ context (crude humour, mild cartoon violence), Rain Dance rhythm play, Ostrich Eggs creep-and-freeze rule, Turtle Shoot description.
- **[Rory's Retrospectives (2013)](https://rorycough24.wordpress.com/2013/07/04/retrospective-no-5-buzz-junior-jungle-party/)** — banana-by-placement session scoring, monkey naming joke, Simian Shootout quick-draw, High Voltage Voting winner-picks-victim rule, Whack A Squirrel rules. → [game-structure](../game-structure.md), [simian-shootout](../simian-shootout.md), [whack-a-squirrel](../whack-a-squirrel.md).

**Anti-source**: avoid [pressstartgaming.com's article](https://pressstartgaming.com/buzz-junior-jungle-party-a-fun-filled-adventure-for-all-ages/) — it names mini-games that do not exist ("Banana Dash", "Coconut Shy"); it reads as AI-generated filler.
