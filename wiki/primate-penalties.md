# Primate Penalties

**Category: Luck** · **Controls: colour buttons** · Longplay chapter 1:15:45 ([watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dZEJTdSRqrY&t=4545s)). Not yet recreated; the only game with its own **named title card in the launch trailer**.

Football penalties, monkey style: the shooter picks a corner, the keeper guesses *(partly inferred)* — a pure matching-pennies mind-game. Also on the ten-game [single-player list](minigames.md#single-player-exclusives).

## What the sources say

- The [launch trailer](sources/launch-trailer.md) devotes two shots to it (0:32–0:35): a leaf-collage **"PRIMATE PENALTIES"** title card, then a **night-time football pitch** with the blue monkey (skull hoodie, red sneakers) sprinting at goal while a **large gorilla goalkeeper** looms, floating button prompts around the frame.
- [Catalog](minigames.md): "Football penalties: shooter picks a corner, keeper guesses *(partly inferred)*."
- German Wikipedia category: **Luck** — consistent with simultaneous corner-guessing.

## Rules *(partly inferred)*

- Shooter secretly picks a target corner/zone on the colour buttons; the keeper (another player? or always the gorilla NPC — the trailer shows a gorilla in goal) picks a zone to dive.
- Mismatch = goal for the shooter; match = save. Players presumably rotate roles *(inferred)*; against the gorilla NPC, saves would be random-weighted *(speculative)*.
- Button prompts in the trailer imply four named zones mapped to the four colours.

## Verification targets for a video pass

Whether players keep goal or the gorilla always does, zones count (4 corners?), shots per player, timing/power elements beyond the guess, scoring.

## Recreation sketch (phone-buzzer fit: excellent)

- Simultaneous hidden picks are tailor-made for phones — each player's choice is genuinely secret (a real advantage over shared-controller originals!). Shooter and keeper both pick a colour zone; reveal together on the TV.
- Rotate all pairings (like our Simian Shootout scheduler); the gorilla keeps goal in odd-player configurations, reusing his `ref` rig.
- Parameter sketch: each player takes 4 shots; goal +100 shooter, save +100 keeper; 5 s pick window, dramatic dive animation either way; night stage flag for the trailer's floodlit mood.
