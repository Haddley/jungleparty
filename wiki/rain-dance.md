# Rain Dance

**Category: Skill** · **Controls: Buzz! button** · Longplay chapter 1:18:34 ([watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dZEJTdSRqrY&t=4714s)). Not yet recreated; core rule from a review.

A rhythm game: hit the buzzer **in time with scrolling icons** to make your monkey dance the rain dance. The confirmed scrolling-cue rhythm entry (its sibling [Monkey Bongo](monkey-bongo.md) is unverified). Also on the ten-game [single-player list](minigames.md#single-player-exclusives).

## Rules (reported / *(inferred)* where noted)

- Icons scroll toward a hit marker; press Buzz! as each icon arrives.
- Hits presumably grade by accuracy and drive the dance animation; misses stumble the dance *(grading and combo details inferred)*.
- Whether all four players share one icon lane or have four parallel lanes is unverified *(four lanes likelier for legibility)*.

## Verification targets for a video pass

Lane layout, icon density/tempo, grading tiers, combo/multiplier, whether the weather visually responds (rain starting as the dance succeeds would be the obvious payoff — check!).

## Recreation sketch (phone-buzzer fit: good)

- Scrolling-lane rhythm on RED, cue times locked to our `MTRACKS` sequencer grid so audio and judgment share a clock; four horizontal lanes stacked on the TV, one per player colour.
- Wide judgment bands for network latency (±120 ms perfect / ±250 ms good), and make the *visual* lane authoritative (players sync to the TV).
- Payoff idea worth stealing regardless of verification: successful streaks summon clouds and rain over your monkey's patch, and the monkey's dance uses `cheer`/`run`/`throw` poses cycled to the beat.
- Parameter sketch: 40 s at ~104 BPM; ~40 cues; perfect 100/good 50; 4-hit streak = rain multiplier ×2.
