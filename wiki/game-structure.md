# Game structure

How a session of Buzz! Junior: Jungle Party is put together, per the [US manual](sources/us-instruction-manual.md) and [reviews](sources/reviews.md).

## Modes

- **Multiplayer** (up to 4 players, one buzzer each) — the main party mode.
- **Single Player** — a run of 10 specially tuned mini-game variants for a total score, chasing the "Jungle Party Hall of Fame" leaderboard; two games (Coconut Catapult, Turtle Shoot) exist only here.
- **Practice** — pick any mini-game freely, solo or multiplayer.
- **Options** — autosave, screen alignment/widescreen, replay intro, leaderboards.

All menus are buzzer-driven, colour-coded to the four buttons (main menu: blue Multiplayer, orange Single Player, green Practice, yellow Options); blue/orange scroll lists and Buzz! confirms. See [buzz-controller](buzz-controller.md#menu-conventions).

## Multiplayer session flow

1. **Length select**: Short / Medium / Long / Marathon (blue/orange/green/yellow). Reported counts differ by region — US 25-game version: 10/15/20/25 games ([Wilcox](sources/reviews.md)); EU 40-game version reported as 5/10/20/40 ([NoobFeed](sources/reviews.md)). *Open item; not yet verified against the [longplay](sources/full-longplay.md).*
2. **Start type**: Quick Start (press your colour to claim a monkey and go), Standard (monkey by seated position + name entry + costume), or Custom (saved playlist of favourites).
3. **Per mini-game**: a narrated [instruction video](sources/instruction-videos.md) plays — any player can skip it with Buzz! — then the game runs, then results.
4. **Session scoring**: mini-games award points internally; across the session players collect **bananas by placement (1st–4th)**, and the most bananas at the end wins the party ([Rory's Retrospectives](sources/reviews.md)). The [announcer](presentation.md#announcer) praises winners and gently mocks losers between games.
5. **Finale**: final standings; multiplayer high scores go on the leaderboard.

## Pause

Hold **all four colour buttons simultaneously** during a mini-game; blue resumes, orange quits to the title screen. ([manual](sources/us-instruction-manual.md#menus-and-modes-as-documented))

## In the recreation

We mirror this loop at party scale: lobby → length select (fewer rounds, drawn from the [starter six](recreation-notes.md#starter-six)) → instruction card (red button skips) → play → placements → banana standings → podium ceremony. Details in [recreation-notes](recreation-notes.md).
