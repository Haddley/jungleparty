# Presentation

Look, sound, and voice of the original — assembled from [reviews](sources/reviews.md) and the [manual](sources/us-instruction-manual.md); to be refined by [video analysis](sources/full-longplay.md).

## Art style

A bright, saturated cartoon jungle. Each mini-game is a self-contained **diorama**: hot-tub clearing, four-lane running track, night-time campfire, river, treetops, small islands, savannah. Wilcox calls the game "a master class in colorful presentation" with environments that feel alive (background critters, swaying foliage); Kikizo notes the "charming graphics and cheeky sense of humour". No board-game hub — one mini-game flows into the next.

## Announcer

A cheerful female voice-over in children's-TV register — "almost condescendingly sweet" (IGN) — who introduces each game, narrates the instruction video, comments on the action, praises winners and consoles/mocks losers, addressing players by their monkey's colour name ("Keep at it, Blue!"). US voice: Becky Boxer; EU English: Liz Barker. The manual's written voice is a different character — the boastful, friendly **gorilla** host. Our recreation uses announcer-style text banners (optionally Web Speech API) with original lines in this register.

## Music

"Driving jungle rhythms, heavy chanting and generally tropical, plucky tunes" (IGN): percussion-forward, upbeat, one consistent style across the game (NoobFeed wished for more variety). Recreation: procedural Web Audio sequencer — djembe-like noise-burst percussion, marimba-ish plucked lead, low chant-like pad — with per-screen tracks and an urgent variant for final seconds.

## Sound effects

Frantic monkey screeches, cartoon boings/bonks/splats, buzzer-confirm blips, explosion booms, and the infamous Bubble Bath bubbling (the ESRB "crude humor"). Recreation SFX are synthesized: pitch-bent oscillator screech, filtered-noise splat, sine-drop bonk, square-wave buzz-in, etc.

## Flow furniture

- Skippable narrated **instruction video** before every game ([instruction videos source](sources/instruction-videos.md)).
- Score presentation between games; **banana standings** for the session ([game-structure](game-structure.md#multiplayer-session-flow)).
- CG intro movie at boot (RealtimeUK), replayable from Options.
