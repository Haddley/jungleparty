**Category: Accuracy** · **Single-player exclusive** · Not in the multiplayer longplay (no chapter timestamp). Not yet recreated; rules *(partly inferred)*.

Solo catapult target practice with coconuts *(partly inferred)* — one of the two [single-player exclusives](minigames.md#single-player-exclusives) (with [Turtle Shoot](turtle-shoot.md)), part of the ten-game single-player mode that feeds the **Jungle Party Hall of Fame** leaderboard ([manual](sources/us-instruction-manual.md)).

## What the sources say

- [2009 Wikipedia list](sources/wikipedia.md) names it among the single-player set; German Wikipedia supplies **Accuracy**.
- The single-player mode context comes from the manual: pick a monkey, name, costume, then play the ten-game set for a total score.
- Presumably shares mechanics with the multiplayer [Monkey Catapult](monkey-catapult.md) — coconuts instead of monkeys as ammunition *(inferred from the names)*.

## Rules *(partly inferred)*

- Launch coconuts at targets with Buzz!-timed inputs (gauge-stop or the [Island War](island-war.md) two-press cycle).
- Score by target value; fixed ammo or time budget for a leaderboard total *(single-player framing implies score-attack structure)*.
