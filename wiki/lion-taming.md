# Lion Taming

**Category: Luck** · **Controls: hold Buzz!** · Longplay chapter 0:57:44 ([watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dZEJTdSRqrY&t=3464s)). Not yet recreated; cited by IGN as a design exemplar, sighted in the trailer.

Head in the lion's mouth: the longer you keep it there, the more points — withdraw before it snaps. IGN's review singles this out (with [Freefall](freefall.md)) as the game's **risk/reward thread**; it is the land-mammal sibling of [Crazy Croc](crazy-croc.md) and the tension template [Bubble Bath](bubble-bath.md) refined.

## Rules (reported / *(inferred)* where noted)

- Hold Buzz! to keep your monkey's head in its lion's mouth; points accrue while held (rate curve unverified — Bubble Bath's verified ramp is the likely template *(inferred)*).
- The lion telegraphs, then snaps; a caught monkey loses its streak (and dignity) *(penalty specifics inferred)*.
- Four lions (trailer) means independent snap timers per player — unlike Bubble Bath's one shared gorilla — so each player manages their own tell *(inferred from staging)*.

## Recreation sketch (phone-buzzer fit: excellent)

- Mechanically our Bubble Bath module with **independent per-player threat timers** — a meaningful variant, not a re-skin: no group-release moments, so it plays as four private games of chicken.
- Lion SVG: mane as a ring of triangles around a face; snap = jaw group rotation; tell = eye open + tail stiffen (~0.8 s, shorter than the gorilla's 1.2 s).
- Parameter sketch: 45 s; snap every 6–15 s per lion; streak-only loss + 2 s recovery; ramp 20→80 pts/s.
