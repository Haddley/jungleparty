# Mini-game catalog

All 40 multiplayer mini-games (EU PS2 version) plus the two single-player exclusives. Names and the game list come from the [2009 Wikipedia revision](sources/wikipedia.md); categories from German Wikipedia; rules assembled from the [manual](sources/us-instruction-manual.md), [reviews](sources/reviews.md), and the [chaptered longplay](sources/full-longplay.md) (timestamp column = where each game appears in that video). Rules marked *(inferred)* are deduced from name/category/fragments and not yet verified on video.

The six **bold** games are our [starter six](recreation-notes.md#starter-six), each with a deep page.

## Single-player exclusives

| Game | Category | Rules |
|---|---|---|
| Coconut Catapult | Accuracy | Solo catapult target practice *(partly inferred)* |
| Turtle Shoot | Accuracy | Drop/pelt coconuts onto menacing tortoises |

Full single-player set of 10: Coconut Catapult, Elephant Baseball, Hippo Splash, Odd One Out, Ostrich Egg, Primate Penalties, Rain Dance, Totem Pole, Turtle Shoot, Whack A Squirrel.

Version subsets: US PS2 has 25 of the 40; PS3 PSN has 5 (list unconfirmed); PSP has 15 (list in [misc sources](sources/misc.md)).
