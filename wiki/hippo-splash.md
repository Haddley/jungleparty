# Hippo Splash

**Category: Memory** · **Controls: colour buttons** · Longplay chapter 0:43:50 ([watch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dZEJTdSRqrY&t=2630s)). Not yet recreated; rules from a launch review.

Spot-the-difference with costumed hippos: a line-up of hippos appears, then one **changes** — press the colour of the changed hippo. Source of two of the game's best-loved NPC designs: the hippo in a **coconut bikini** and the one in a **cowboy hat** ([monkeys](monkeys.md) NPC list).

## What the sources say

- [Kikizo](sources/reviews.md) documents the spot-the-difference mechanic in its launch review.
- [Catalog](minigames.md): "Costumed hippos appear, then one changes; press the colour of the changed hippo."
- The [launch trailer](sources/launch-trailer.md) Odd One Out shot (0:50–0:51) shows hippos in pink tutus — costumed hippos are evidently a shared asset between the two quiz games.
- Also on the ten-game [single-player list](minigames.md#single-player-exclusives). German Wikipedia category: **Memory**.

## Rules (reported / *(inferred)* where noted)

- Four hippos, one per colour, pose in costumes; players study them.
- After a beat (curtain, dive underwater, or splash — the transition is unverified *(inferred)*), one hippo resurfaces changed (different hat, accessory, or costume piece).
- Fastest player to press the changed hippo's colour scores; multiple rounds of presumably escalating subtlety *(inferred)*.

## Verification targets for a video pass

The hide/reveal transition, what kinds of changes occur (added, removed, swapped items), how subtle late rounds get, scoring and wrong-guess penalty.

## Recreation sketch (phone-buzzer fit: excellent)

- Pure colour-button quiz — direct port. Build hippos as a paper-doll SVG (base hippo + hat/glasses/bikini/bowtie slots) so "one change" is a one-slot swap; that also gives us the coconut-bikini and cowboy-hat cameos.
- Difficulty ramp: early rounds swap loud items (hat appears), late rounds swap subtle ones (bowtie colour).
- Parameter sketch: 8 rounds; study 4 s → splash 1 s → answer window 6 s; +100 fastest correct, wrong = 1.5 s lockout.
